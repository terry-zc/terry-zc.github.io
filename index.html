    Bio

    I am currently a Research Assistant of Shenzhen Institute of Data Economy(SIDE), which is affiliated with the Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen. In this role, I am involved in cutting-edge research projects across various areas, including agricultural operation management and industrial supply chain management. Prior to my research position, I gained valuable experience as a Data Analyst at OPPO, where I played a pivotal role in driving data-driven strategies for digital marketing initiatives. My responsibilities included collecting and analyzing complex datasets, and identifying key marketing trends.

    I am actively seeking opportunities to pursue a PhD in Operation Management, Information Systems, or Supply Chain Management. I aspire to contribute to the advancement of knowledge in my field through rigorous research, proficient data analysis skills, and cross-disciplinary collaboration. My passion lies in leveraging data to create positive societal impact.

    I am open to exciting research positions and collaborative opportunities. Let's connect and explore the possibilities together!
